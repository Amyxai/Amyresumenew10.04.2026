Head of Operations · Chief of Staff
Diesel & Oils Tanker Transport

9 Years · Sole Operator Fleet: 200 Units 3 Business Entities Transport · Fuel Distribution · Mining

Joined as the sole operational and executive support function for a single-entity road transport business with a small filling station. Over nine years, architected and implemented the full operational, compliance, financial, and people infrastructure that directly enabled the business to grow from one entity into three — expanding from a transport operation into a 200-unit tanker fleet, a fully compliant diesel depot, and an acquired manganese mining operation. Business Growth & Operational Transformation

  • Joined a single transport business with a small filling station and no formalised operational systems, compliance frameworks, or performance management infrastructure in place.
  • Designed and implemented Invesstock Managed Solutions — a proprietary, in-house operational management system built from scratch to integrate and oversee all three business entities within a single operational framework, covering fleet, depot, and mine operations concurrently.
  • The introduction of structured KPI frameworks, compliance programmes, and the Invesstock Managed Solutions system directly contributed to measurable business growth: the filling station scaled into a fully compliant diesel depot, the manganese mine was subsequently acquired, the tanker fleet doubled to 200 units, and overall business turnover increased significantly across the tenure period.
  • Driver attrition reduced materially following implementation of structured induction programmes, driver training initiatives, and performance management frameworks.
  • Mine safety incidents maintained a near-zero scoring record throughout the full operational period — reflecting the effectiveness of hazard management, inspection, and audit systems implemented.
  • Diesel pricing compliance and cost index alignment maintained continuously, with zero pricing audit failures recorded across the depot's COD sales model.
02

Systems, Technology & Compliance Infrastructure

  • Designed and built Invesstock Managed Solutions from the ground up — a bespoke in-house operational management system integrating oversight across all three business entities without reliance on external consultants or off-the-shelf frameworks.
  • Developed a proprietary internal audit and compliance management system incorporating weekly operational checks across all three sites — proactively surfacing gaps, assigning corrective actions, and maintaining continuous audit-readiness at all times.
  • Designed and implemented a KPI monitoring programme across all operational sites — tracking performance metrics, identifying variances, and generating structured performance reporting for the Director at every month-end cycle.
  • Administered all financial management and accounting through Xero — covering invoicing, reconciliations, financial reporting, and month-end close processes across the full business.
  • Managed two distinct month-end billing cycles for the tanker fleet operation — auditing and signing off all cycle statements and ensuring financial reporting was accurate and board-ready.
